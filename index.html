Why NaloxNow?

Life-Saving Technology

NaloxNow addresses one of Canada's most urgent public health challenges by providing real-time access to naloxone kits, empowering communities to take immediate action during overdose emergencies.

  • Supporting At-Risk Individuals – Rapid access to emergency resources
  • Emergency Navigation – Find and reach naloxone kits quickly
  • AI-Powered Assistance – Real-time guidance on overdose response
  • Community Support – Connect with emergency contacts instantly

Emergency Button Workflow

1. User Triggers Emergency Alert

Action: The user presses the "Need Help Now" button in the app.
  • App retrieves the user's live location via GPS.
  • A confirmation pop-up appears (optional, if accidental press needs to be avoided).
  • The app immediately sends data to Google Workflows to begin the emergency process.

2. Contact Emergency Contacts

Action: Google Workflows sends an automated alert to all of the user's saved emergency contacts.
  • A text message (SMS) & email is sent to pre-saved emergency contacts with:
    • Live GPS location link
    • Message: "[User's Name] has triggered an emergency alert and may need immediate assistance. Click the link for their real-time location."
  • Optional: Push notification to contacts who also have the NaloxNow app.

3. Call 911 & Share Live Location

Action: The app automatically dials 911 and shares the user's location.
  • A Google Cloud Function triggers an automated voice call to 911.
  • Text-to-speech message plays:
    • "This is an automated emergency alert from the NaloxNow app. A user has triggered an overdose alert at [live location]. Please send immediate assistance."
  • If the user cannot speak, the dispatcher already has the live location link.

4. Display On-Screen Overdose Response Guidance

Action: The app provides immediate, step-by-step instructions on how to handle an overdose situation.
  • The AI Chatbot opens automatically and provides:
    • Naloxone usage instructions
    • Basic first-aid steps
    • What to do while waiting for help
  • If others are nearby, they can follow the steps while help arrives.
