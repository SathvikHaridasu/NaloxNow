What You Need to Know

Emergency Response

Naloxone kits are available 24/7 at many locations. In an emergency, call 911 immediately and administer naloxone if available.

Free Access

Naloxone is available without a prescription in Canada. Many pharmacies and health centers provide it free of charge.

Community Support

Local organizations and health departments often have naloxone distribution programs and training available.

Additional Resources

Contact your local health department or visit naloxone.ca for more information about availability in your area.
